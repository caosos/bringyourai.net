The Mission

Bring Your AI is the idea that personal AI should move with the person, respect the person, and operate under the person’s rules. The future is not one chatbot trapped in one website. The future is governed AI that can support real workflows across tools, devices, environments, and time.

User-Owned Context

Memory should be explicit, inspectable, portable, correctable, and governed by the user.

Operational Workflows

AI should help with real tasks: maintenance, documentation, reminders, care workflows, planning, and technical reasoning.

Device-Aware AI

Voice, wearables, tablets, sensors, and building systems can become part of a human-centered assistance layer.

CAOS

CAOS Care is the senior-care application layer: practical AI support for residents, staff, reminders, alerts, documentation, and assistive workflows. It is intended as a human-supervised support layer, not an autonomous medical decision-maker.
